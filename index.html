Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the DevFlow ecosystem.

What is DevFlow exactly?

DevFlow is a unified native desktop application that consolidates your development workflow. It brings together repository management, AI-driven debugging, deployment monitoring, and secret storage into a single, high-performance interface.

Is DevFlow free to use?

Yes! The core DevFlow dashboard is completely free and open-source. We also offer a "DevFlow Pro" tier for teams that includes advanced collaboration features and priority AI assistance.

Which platforms are supported?

Currently, DevFlow is available as a native app for macOS (Intel and Apple Silicon) and Windows 10/11. A Linux version is currently in early beta and will be released soon.

How does the AI assistant work?

The AI assistant scans your local build logs and code context to identify errors in real-time. It uses Claude and GPT-4o to suggest precise fixes, so you spend less time searching StackOverflow and more time building.

Is my data secure?

Security is our top priority. Your environment variables and secrets are encrypted with AES-256 and stored locally on your machine. We never upload your sensitive data to our servers.
